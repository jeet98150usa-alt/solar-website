Panel Comparison

MONOFACIAL VS
BIFACIAL PANELS.

Choose the right panel technology for your installation environment and project scale.

MONOFACIAL
Front-side only · ~18–22% efficiency
  • Generates electricity from front side only
  • Standard roof-mount — facing the sun
  • Lower cost · Widely used in homes
  • Suitable for homes and budget projects
Ideal for Homes & Small Projects
BIFACIAL
Dual-side · 5–30% more output
  • Captures light from both front and rear
  • Harvests reflected light from ground & rooftop
  • 5–30% higher energy output
  • Ideal for villas, farms & advanced projects
Best for Large / Advanced Projects
System Modes

CHOOSE YOUR SOLAR SETUP.

We design and install all three system types — tailored to your power needs, location, and budget. Every home is different; your system should be too.

01
🏙️

ON-GRID SYSTEM

Panels generate DC converted to AC by the inverter. Extra power exports to the utility grid via net metering — saving you money automatically.

Lower installation cost
Lower maintenance
Net metering savings
Best for stable grid areas
02
🏕️

OFF-GRID SYSTEM

Fully independent — no grid connection needed. Solar charges batteries via charge controller. Stored energy powers your home round the clock.

Power in remote areas
Total energy independence
No grid dependency
Ideal for rural homes
03
⚡

HYBRID SYSTEM

The best of both worlds — powers loads, charges batteries, and exports surplus to the grid. Auto-switches for 24/7 uninterrupted power.

24/7 power availability
Export extra units to grid
Backup during outages
Ideal for independent houses
